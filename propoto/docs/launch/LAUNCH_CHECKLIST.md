## 3. Launch Day Checklist

### 3.1 Launch Day Schedule (Tuesday)

| Time (PT) | Activity | Owner |
|-----------|----------|-------|
| 12:00 AM | Product Hunt goes live | Hunter |
| 12:05 AM | Post first comment | Founder |
| 12:15 AM | Social media posts go live | Marketing |
| 6:00 AM | Morning check: PH rank, signups | Founder |
| 9:00 AM | Email blast to subscribers | Marketing |
| 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM | Monitor & respond to PH comments | All hands |
| 12:00 PM | Midday update post (social) | Founder |
| 3:00 PM | Afternoon check: metrics | Founder |
| 6:00 PM | End of day wrap-up | Founder |
| 8:00 PM | Thank you post (social) | Founder |

### 3.2 Product Hunt Actions

**Every hour:**
- [ ] Check ranking
- [ ] Respond to all new comments (< 30 min)
- [ ] Thank upvoters who comment
- [ ] Share updates if hitting milestones

**Comment response priorities:**
1. Questions about the product (answer immediately)
2. Feature requests (acknowledge + roadmap)
3. Compliments (thank + ask for follow)
4. Comparisons (honest, non-defensive)
5. Skepticism (address with evidence)

### 3.3 Social Media Actions

**Twitter/X:**
- [ ] Launch announcement thread
- [ ] Reply to every comment
- [ ] Retweet supporter posts
- [ ] Share PH link in bio

**LinkedIn:**
- [ ] Launch announcement post
- [ ] Engage with all comments
- [ ] DM supporters to thank them

**Discord:**
- [ ] Launch announcement
- [ ] Live updates on progress
- [ ] Celebrate milestones together

### 3.4 Emergency Protocols

| Issue | Response |
|-------|----------|
| **Site down** | Immediately post on PH, Twitter. All hands on fix. |
| **Major bug** | Acknowledge publicly, fix ASAP, update when resolved. |
| **Negative review** | Respond professionally, offer to make it right. |
| **Overwhelmed by demand** | Happy problem! Communicate delays, thank for patience. |

## 7. Contingency Plans

### 7.1 If Launch Doesn't Hit Goals

**If < 100 signups:**
- Analyze traffic sources
- Check conversion on landing page
- A/B test messaging
- Increase outreach efforts
- Consider paid promotion

**If < 5 paid:**
- Review pricing perception
- Check trial experience
- Increase personal outreach
- Offer extended trials
- Gather feedback on blockers

### 7.2 If Launch Exceeds Capacity

**If servers struggling:**
- Enable queue system
- Scale infrastructure
- Communicate delays
- Prioritize paid users

**If support overwhelmed:**
- Prioritize paying customers
- Use templates for common questions
- Point to self-serve resources
- Set expectations on response time
