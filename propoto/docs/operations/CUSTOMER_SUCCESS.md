# Propoto Customer Success Playbook

**Owner:** Customer Success Lead  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** December 2025

---

## 1. Customer Success Philosophy

**Mission:** Make every Propoto customer wildly successful at closing deals.

**Principles:**
- Proactive > Reactive
- Outcomes > Features
- Speed to value > Everything else
- Every customer is a potential case study

**Success Definition:** Customer generates 10+ proposals/month AND reports time savings.

## 2. Customer Lifecycle

```
Signup → Activation → Engagement → Retention → Expansion → Advocacy
   ↓         ↓            ↓            ↓           ↓           ↓
 Day 0    Day 1-3      Day 7-30     Day 30+    Month 3+    Month 6+
```

### 2.1 Stage Definitions

| Stage | Definition | Key Metric |
|-------|------------|------------|
| **Signup** | Account created | Trial started |
| **Activation** | First "aha" moment | 3+ proposals generated |
| **Engagement** | Regular usage | Weekly proposals |
| **Retention** | Continued payment | No churn signals |
| **Expansion** | Increased usage/spend | Upgrade or add seats |
| **Advocacy** | Active promotion | Referrals, testimonials |

## 3. Onboarding

### 3.1 Onboarding Goals

| Goal | Target | Measurement |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Time to first proposal | < 5 minutes | From signup |
| Proposals in first week | 3+ | Usage tracking |
| Onboarding completion | 80% | Checklist completion |

### 3.2 Onboarding Checklist

**Day 0: Welcome**
- [ ] Welcome email sent (automated)
- [ ] Account setup guide provided
- [ ] Quick start video linked

**Day 1: First Value**
- [ ] First proposal generated
- [ ] Deck viewed
- [ ] Export tested (PDF/PPTX)

**Day 3: Deeper Usage**
- [ ] Template explored
- [ ] Brand voice configured (if Growth+)
- [ ] Second proposal generated

**Day 7: Activation Check**
- [ ] 3+ proposals generated
- [ ] Personal check-in (high-value accounts)
- [ ] Feature discovery email sent

### 3.3 Onboarding Email Sequence

| Day | Email | Goal |
|-----|-------|------|
| 0 | "Welcome to Propoto — generate your first proposal" | Activation |
| 1 | "Did you try [feature]?" | Feature discovery |
| 3 | "How [Agency] saved 15 hours/week" | Social proof |
| 5 | "5 tips to get more from Propoto" | Education |
| 7 | "How's it going?" (from founder) | Personal touch |
| 10 | "Your trial: halfway there" | Urgency |
| 12 | "What happens when your trial ends" | Conversion prep |
| 14 | "Last day + special offer" | Conversion |

### 3.4 High-Touch Onboarding (Agency+ Tier)

**Onboarding Call Agenda (30 min):**
1. Introductions (2 min)
2. Goals: What does success look like? (5 min)
3. Current process: How do you do proposals today? (5 min)
4. Walkthrough: Live demo with their data (10 min)
5. Brand voice setup: Configure their voice (5 min)
6. Next steps: 30-day success plan (3 min)

## 5. Retention & Churn Prevention

### 5.1 Churn Reasons (from interviews)

| Reason | Frequency | Prevention |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| "Not using it enough" | 35% | Usage triggers, check-ins |
| "Budget cuts" | 25% | ROI proof, downgrade option |
| "Missing features" | 20% | Roadmap communication |
| "Quality issues" | 15% | Feedback loop, fixes |
| "Found alternative" | 5% | Competitive awareness |

### 5.2 Save Plays

**When customer says "I want to cancel":**

1. **Acknowledge:** "I'm sorry to hear that. What's prompting this?"
2. **Listen:** Let them explain fully
3. **Respond based on reason:**

| Reason | Save Play |
|--------|-----------|
| "Not using it" | "Let's do a 15-min call to help you integrate it into your workflow. Free reset." |
| "Too expensive" | "Would a downgrade to Starter work? You'd keep core features at $99/mo." |
| "Missing feature" | "That's on our roadmap for [date]. Can you hold on until then?" |
| "Quality issues" | "I'd love to understand specifics. Can we schedule a call to make this right?" |
| "Found something else" | "Respect that. Mind sharing what's better? We'd love to improve." |

4. **Last resort:** "Can I pause your account for 30 days instead of canceling?"

## 7. Advocacy

### 7.1 Advocacy Program

**Tiers:**
1. **Reviewer:** Leaves G2/Capterra review → Free month
2. **Reference:** Takes reference calls → Propoto swag
3. **Case Study:** Featured story → Free quarter + co-marketing
4. **Ambassador:** Ongoing promotion → Revenue share / affiliate

### 7.2 Testimonial Collection

**When to ask:**
- After first successful proposal sent
- After positive support interaction
- At renewal (if healthy)
- After feature request is shipped

**Template:**
> "Hey [Name], I noticed you've generated [X] proposals this month — that's awesome! Would you be open to sharing a quick quote about your experience with Propoto? Just 1-2 sentences about what's working for you."

### 7.3 Case Study Process

1. **Identify candidates:** High usage + positive feedback
2. **Initial ask:** "Would you be open to being featured?"
3. **Interview:** 30-min call about their results
4. **Draft:** Write case study, send for approval
5. **Publish:** Blog, website, sales collateral
6. **Reward:** Free quarter + social media feature

### 8.3 Common Issues & Resolutions

| Issue | Resolution | Prevention |
|-------|------------|------------|
| Gamma deck not loading | Retry, check Gamma status | Status page |
| Proposal too generic | Enable deep scraping, provide more context | Better onboarding |
| Wrong pricing format | Edit output, adjust prompt | Template improvement |
| Slow generation | Check model, retry | Performance monitoring |
| Login issues | Password reset, Clerk support | Better error messages |

### 8.4 Escalation Matrix

| Level | Owner | Criteria |
|-------|-------|----------|
| L1 | Support agent | Standard issues |
